Who I Am

My name is Mouhameth Ba. I am a Mechatronics and Robotics Systems Engineering student with a concentration in Electrical Engineering, currently working as a Mechatronics & Robotics Technician on the Amazon Robotics account through CBRE.

I work daily with automated systems, robotics, conveyors, sensors, and electrical equipment in a high-availability industrial environment. My background combines hands-on troubleshooting with a growing foundation in engineering theory, programming, and system design.
